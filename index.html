My Book Corner!


  • Mistborn

Notes: This book has a really good fantasy setting with interesting characters and a unqiue magic system. The world feel very lived and makes you want to learn more about it. The book also uses its unqiue magic system to make elaborate, tense, and fun fight scenes. Solid 9/10

  • Percy Jacson

Notes: This book blends the mytholigcal with the real in many intersting, and often quite clever, ways. The characters are all lovable and the plot is very intersting, with some great twists. This book also serves as a great introduction for all the future books in the series. 9/10

  • I am Number Four

Notes: This book has a really interesting magic system and great fight scenes. Characters feel very real and they all have great chemistry with one another. The overarching plot creates a tense atmosphere that makes you always want to know what happens next. 9.5/10

  • The Hidden Oracle

Notes: This book is another Rick Riordan book. This book focus on Apollo and explores his personality in greater depth than ever before. This book also explores how he grows now that he is stuck here on Earth. 8.5/10

  • An Abundance of Katherines

Notes: This one is probably my favorite book on this list. It has a plethora of interesting characters that have great chemistry with one another. Of all the good characters, the relationships between the main trio is fun and very engaging. 10/10
